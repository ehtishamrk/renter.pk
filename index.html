About Renter

Transforming how Pakistan rents since 2024

We're Building Pakistan's Rental Revolution

Founded in 2024, Renter was born from a simple observation: finding quality rentals in Pakistan was unnecessarily difficult. Whether it was apartments, vehicles, tools, or even designer clothes, the process was fragmented, untrustworthy, and frustrating.

We set out to change that. Today, Renter is Pakistan's premier rental marketplace, connecting millions of renters with verified listings across multiple categories.
